 4. How to use APP to monitor ECG and generate viewing reports?     

    
 

15 minutes free report generation and viewing:  

 

(1) Make sure the Bluetooth and ECG power on the phone are turned on.  

 

(2) Correctly wear the electrocardiograph  

 

(3) Login to Bisha Kangjian APP to enter all devices interface  

 
 
 

(4) Click on the bundled ECG products in the list to enter the ECG monitoring interface.  

 
 
 

(5) Click the Generate Report button in the lower left corner to select the type of report to be generated (charge report needs to be recharged in advance)  

 
 

(6) Click to start the monitoring button and continue to wear the electrocardiograph according to the selected report monitoring duration.  

 
 
 

(7) The report will be generated and pushed to you after the monitoring is finished. You can also generate and view the report immediately by clicking the Generate Now button in the lower right corner.  

 
 
 

(8) You can also view the historical report by clicking the View Report button in the lower right corner of the ECG monitoring interface.  

 

24-hour charge report generation and viewing (charge report needs to be recharged in advance)  

 

(1) Make sure the Bluetooth and ECG power on the phone are turned on.  

 

(2) Keep the correct wearing for up to 24 hours (the report can be generated after collecting data for less than 24 hours. It is recommended to ensure that the electrocardiograph is fully charged before wearing)  

 

(3) Login to Bisha Kangjian APP to enter all devices interface  

 
 
 

(4) Click on the ECG product that has been bound in the list to enter the ECG monitoring interface.  

 
 
 

(5) Use the OTG data cable to connect the mobile phone to the ECG device and wait for the precautions. After confirming, enter the selection data interface. If no prompts pop up, please click the Generate Report button in the lower left corner after connecting to select to generate the 24-hour charge report. And start monitoring.  

 
 

(6) Select the ECG data needed to generate reports in the selected data interface (ECG data are arranged according to date and time, only one time period can be selected).

(7) Click the report button in the lower right corner to upload data, and all operations are completed. (Fee report needs a certain time for manual review, and will be automatically pushed to you after completion, please wait patiently).

Guidelines for the Use of ECG

1) Installation of disposable ECG electrodes

A closed dynamic ECG recorder was taken out, and the protective film of the electrode sheet was removed by aligning the electrode sheet with the four corners of the dynamic ECG recorder.

2) wearing dynamic ECG recorder

It is suggested to shave the body hair at the location of the electrodes (red ellipse area), thoroughly clean the grease and dirt on the skin where the electrodes are installed, keep the operation keys of the dynamic ECG recorder facing upward, tightly adhere the electrodes to the skin by one-time use, and stick the electrodes to the position specified by the ellipse circle in the picture. Sit close to both sides of the heart socket.

3) Start collecting data

Start the dynamic ECG recorder, press the operation key for a long time until the indicator is green. After a few seconds, the indicator begins to flicker and the data acquisition begins.

4) Ending Data Acquisition

When the data acquisition is finished (usually 24 hours), turn off the dynamic ECG recorder and press the operation key for a long time until the indicator is green. After a few seconds, the indicator is turned off and the acquisition data is finished.

5) Remove the dynamic ECG recorder

After collecting the data, hold the dynamic ECG recorder with your hand. As shown in the figure below, start at point A of the electrodes, then slowly uncover the next time using the electrodes from outside to inside, put down the dynamic ECG recorder, then pull out the electrodes and discard the electrodes in the garbage bin. Do not tear the electrodes forcefully when taking them off, so as not to damage the skin.

6) Upload data

Connect the dynamic ECG recorder to the mobile phone through OTG data line or to the computer through USB data line. Refer to the latest instructions of our company's official website for the data of the dynamic ECG recorder.

7) Emergency help-seeking operation

In the acquisition state, the dynamic electrocardiograph continuously presses 2 operation keys to trigger the active help, the buzzer will sound 3 times in 2 seconds, and send the SMS to the emergency contact (in the case of customized app service). At this time, press the operation key again to cancel the call for help, and the buzzer stops calling.

8) Cardiac reminder

In the acquisition state, if the user has a cardiac arrest event, the dynamic ECG recorder will trigger a cardiac arrest for help. The buzzer will buzz three times in about 2 seconds and send short messages to emergency contacts (in the case of customized APP service).

** Our disposable electrocardiogram patches have passed the biocompatibility test. If skin discomfort or swelling occurs after wearing disposable electrocardiogram patches, please stop using them immediately and remove the dynamic electrocardiogram recorder. If necessary, please go to the hospital for medical treatment.

State description of dynamic ECG recorder

Dynamic ECG recorder has three states: working state, closed state, charging/USB connection state. When the dynamic ECG recorder is in the closed state, press the operation key for about 3 seconds, and the green light for about 5 seconds, and then enter the working state. When the dynamic ECG recorder is in working state, press the operation key for about 3 seconds, the green light will be on for 3 to 5 seconds, and then enter the closed state. When the dynamic ECG recorder is in working state or closed state, connecting the boot computer with USB data line will enter the charging/USB connection state. Dynamic ECG recorder can not collect ECG data under charging/USB connection.

Apple User Report Generation Guide

Apple User Report Generation Guide

1.使用 micro USB 將監測完畢的 心電記錄儀 與 電腦 連接。

2. 打開 我的電腦。

3.打開 HC3A250 盤（如找不到HC3A250盤，可能是記錄儀與電腦連接失敗，請再次拔插嘗試連接）。

4.進入 USDATA 文件夾。

5.根據文件名中的日期時間（紅線標記部分）找出需要上傳的ECD文件。

6.右鍵 ECD文件 ，在菜單中點擊複制（紅線標記部分）。

7.回到 桌面 右鍵桌面，在菜單中點擊粘貼（紅線標記部分）。

8.使用浏覽器進入 https://hk-server.bisahealth .com/login

9.使用賬號密碼登錄（忘記密碼可使用免密登錄）。

10.在左側菜單中點擊 報告管理 。

11.點擊 新建報告 按鈕打開創建報告界面。

12.點擊 上傳ECD 按鈕（紅框標記部分）打開文件選擇框。

13.點擊左側 桌面（紅框標記部分） 後找出並選中之前粘貼的ECD文件，點擊右下角 打開 按鈕。

14.填寫 姓名、年齡、性別等必選項 ，選填其他內容。

15.點擊 創建報告 按鈕（紅框標記部分），用戶完成所有上傳操作。

16.等待報告生成與查看報告生成狀態（48小時內生成，請留意報告狀態）。

17.報告狀態處于 待查看 時，用戶可查看、下載報告。

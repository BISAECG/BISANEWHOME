Delivery time

We will issue the order within 7 days with reference to the payment order. In the shortest case, our products will be shipped out within 24 hours. Due to objective logistics factors, there may be some delay. Please be patient.

Note: Express delivery may be delayed due to traffic, weather, etc. It is recommended that you visit the well-being health mall, go to the order details or the delivery company's official website to pay attention to the delivery progress.

If you have more than one item in the same order, the estimated delivery time of the order depends on the item with the longest expected delivery time in the order. We will not split multiple orders and issue them separately. The package product is processed in the same way as multiple product orders and will not be shipped separately.
